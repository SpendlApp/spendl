Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Spendl operates the Spendl mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how Spendl handles information when you use the App.

Summary

Spendl is built with privacy in mind. Your subscription data is stored on your device and, if you use iCloud, synced to your personal iCloud account. Spendl does not operate servers that collect or store your personal subscription data.

Information Spendl does not collect

  • Spendl does not require you to create an account.
  • Spendl does not collect your subscription names, amounts, or renewal dates on its servers.
  • Spendl does not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Spendl does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Information stored on your device

Spendl stores the subscription information you enter (such as names, amounts, renewal dates, categories, and notes) locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you are signed into iCloud and have iCloud sync enabled, this data may be synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Information processed by third parties

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — If you purchase Spendl Pro, payment and subscription management are handled entirely by Apple. Spendl does not receive your payment card details.
  • Exchange rate API — Spendl Pro's multi-currency feature fetches public exchange rates from open.er-api.com. Only currency codes are sent in these requests; no personal subscription data is included.
  • Apple Push Notification Service — Renewal reminders are scheduled locally on your device. No notification content is sent to Spendl's servers.

Face ID and passcode

If you enable App Lock, authentication is handled entirely by your device's secure enclave via Apple's LocalAuthentication framework. Spendl does not receive or store your biometric data.

Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall Spendl. Deleting the app removes locally stored data. iCloud-synced data can be managed through your iCloud settings.

Children's privacy

Spendl is not directed at children under 13. Spendl does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

Spendl may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact Spendl at support.spendl@gmail.com.
